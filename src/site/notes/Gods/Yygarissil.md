# Yygarissil
 [[Gods/Yygarissil\|Yygarissil]] is common name for the location that gods ascend to. It is unknown whether this place exists in a parallel plane or is outside of [[World (Erodas)/Erodas\|Erodas]]. It is believed that essences of life have come from this place and is a place of undeath. 

In [[World (Erodas)/Korlornium/Korlornium\|Korlornium]] people have claimed to see visions of this place particularly near [[World (Erodas)/Korlornium/Mount Redshield\|Mount Redshield]] during [[Winter's Crest\|Winter's Crest]].

[[Gods/Major Powers/Ulthagos\|Ulthagos]] was cursed to have to carry the burden of sculpting the material world and is not in [[Gods/Yygarissil\|Yygarissil]]
