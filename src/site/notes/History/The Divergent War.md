# The Divergent War
[[History/The Divergent War\|The Divergent War]] refers to the war which followed [[History/The Red Storm\|The Red Storm]]. It was named the [[History/The Divergent War\|The Divergent War]] because it politically separated [[World (Erodas)/Korlornium/Siroth/Siroth\|Siroth]] and [[World (Erodas)/Korlornium/Caldrith\|Caldrith]].

The war was between the religious entities of [[World (Erodas)/Korlornium/Dithoria City/Dithoria City\|Dithoria City]], the militia of [[Organizations/The Rellian Dynasty\|The Rellian Dynasty]], and the [[Siroth Empire\|Siroth Empire]].

[[Organizations/The Rellian Dynasty\|The Rellian Dynasty]] tried to control all of [[World (Erodas)/Korlornium/Korlornium\|Korlornium]] through military force. It came to a conclusion as religious forces from [[World (Erodas)/Korlornium/Dithoria City/Dithoria City\|Dithoria City]] pushed [[Organizations/The Rellian Dynasty\|Rellian]] troops out of [[Wyegate\|Wyegate]] and united northern [[World (Erodas)/Korlornium/Caldrith\|Caldrith]] under the [[Organizations/The Righteous Banner\|The Righteous Banner]].
