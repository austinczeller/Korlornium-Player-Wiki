# [[Calendar/Months/Xhaale (Exhale)\|Xhaale (Exhale)]]
## Description
[[Calendar/Months/Xhaale (Exhale)\|Xhaale (Exhale)]] is the third month of the Erodian Year. It represents the [[Species/Common Intelligent Species/Elf\|Elvish]] expiration and decay of life. In [[Northern Erodas\|Northern Erodas]] it is the month of Fall.
