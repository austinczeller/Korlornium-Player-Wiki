# [[Calendar/(10) Vaelchron\|(10) Vaelchron]]
## Description
The tenth and final day of the week representing the [[Species/Common Intelligent Species/Elf\|Elvish]] time.

[[Calendar/(10) Vaelchron\|(10) Vaelchron]] is the longest day of the week with an additional 3 extra hours.
During this day most shops are closed and used as a day to rest.
