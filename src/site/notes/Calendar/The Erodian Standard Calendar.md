# [[Calendar/The Erodian Standard Calendar\|The Erodian Standard Calendar]]
## Description
[[Calendar/The Erodian Standard Calendar\|The Erodian Standard Calendar]] was a calendar developed by the [[Organizations/The Erodian Atlasing Society\|The Erodian Atlasing Society]] to help catalog the sequence of events occurring on the planet [[World (Erodas)/Erodas\|Erodas]]. Although not prefect at representing seasonal changes in [[Southern Erodas\|Southern Erodas]] the calendar was created to serve the majority of the planet's population in [[Northern Erodas\|Northern Erodas]]. The Erodian Year starts in [[Calendar/Months/Nhaale (Inhale)\|Nhaale (Inhale)]] and ends in [[Calendar/Months/Salaale (Quiet)\|Salaale (Quiet)]]. [[Calendar/The Erodian Standard Calendar\|The Erodian Standard Calendar]] The year subsequent to the creation of [[World (Erodas)/Korlornium/The Scar Sea/The Scar\|The Scar]] in [[World (Erodas)/Korlornium/Korlornium\|Korlornium]], as this was a sizeable seismic, cultural and celestial phenomena. Years prior are refereed to as a negative number or through the term *Pre-Scar*.

This calendar seasonally groups the four months:
- Spring: [[Calendar/Months/Nhaale (Inhale)\|Nhaale (Inhale)]]
- Summer: [[Calendar/Months/Htaale (Heat)\|Htaale (Heat)]]
- Autumn: [[Calendar/Months/Xhaale (Exhale)\|Xhaale (Exhale)]]
- Winter: [[Calendar/Months/Salaale (Quiet)\|Salaale (Quiet)]]
A month has 10 weeks of 10 days resembling an [[Species/Common Intelligent Species/Elf\|Elvish]] element.

Weekdays are as follows: 
- [[Calendar/(1) Vitael\|(1) Vitael]]
- [[Calendar/(2) Luxael\|(2) Luxael]]
- [[Calendar/(3) Umrael\|(3) Umrael]]
- [[Calendar/(4) Terrael\|(4) Terrael]]
- [[Calendar/(5) Aelwyn\|(5) Aelwyn]]
- [[Calendar/(6) Aquael\|(6) Aquael]]
- [[Calendar/(7) Pyrael\|(7) Pyrael]]
- [[Calendar/(8) Temprael\|(8) Temprael]]
- [[Calendar/(9) Veloriael\|(9) Veloriael]]
- [[Calendar/(10) Vaelchron\|(10) Vaelchron]]

Each month is equal length (80 days) regardless regional seasonal differences. 
Creating a yearly calendar of 320 total days.
