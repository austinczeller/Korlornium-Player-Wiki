# The Righteous Banner
[[Organizations/The Righteous Banner\|The Righteous Banner]] is the name of the military brigade rallied during [[History/The Divergent War\|The Divergent War]] blessed by [[Gods/Major Powers/Dithoria (God)\|Dithoria (God)]] to keep his kingdom safe. Ultimately, [[Organizations/The Righteous Banner\|The Righteous Banner]] defeated much of the [[Organizations/The Rellian Dynasty\|Rellian]] troops at the [[Battle of Wyegate\|Battle of Wyegate]]. [[Organizations/The Righteous Banner\|The Righteous Banner]] currently still controls much [[World (Erodas)/Korlornium/Caldrith\|Caldrith]]'s northeastern land, except for the free city of [[Konduit\|Konduit]].
Commanded by [[NPC's/Inactive or Perished/Hethul, The Righteous\|Hethul, The Righteous]] during battles.
